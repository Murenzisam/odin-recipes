Pilau

a rice dish, or in some regions, a wheat dish, whose recipe usually involves cooking in stock or broth, adding spices, and other ingredients such as vegetables or meat.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Pilau masala
  • Curry powder

    • Steps

  • You need to add the appropriate amount of water so that rice isn't overly cooked or undercooked
  • After that you add some oil in the rice
  • Then you add some Pilau masala, 3 spoons will do.
  • Then you add cooked meat to the dish
  • You then add some onions to make it have some smell and taste
  • After that you add some curry powder, 2 spoons
  • Last but not least you add some salt. That's it
